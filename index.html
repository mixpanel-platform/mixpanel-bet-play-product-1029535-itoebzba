Glossary: Metrics Definitions

Event Name Type Definition
App Opened This event is triggered on app cold launch, when an app is opened via deeplink, and when an app is foregrounded after 5 minutes (which launches a new app session)
Free Trial Initiated Triggered on every free trial, and more specifically when a user encounters the 'Trial Success Screen' on the app after successfully authenticating on the app store
Franchise View Open Triggered when a user taps on a Franchise/Show from the lobby
Video Started Fired when a video starts playing, whether through autoplay, deeplink or a click event
Video Watched Fired when the play screen or view screen stops or changes, which may be when the app is backgrounded, when a video complete with autoplay is disabled, or when a video completes and user moves on to next video with autoplay enabled
Franchise View Closed Fired when user closes the franchise/show's episode view list
Session Denotes when a user's attention shifts away from the app and finalizes their visit, namely when the user force closes the app, or after the user backgrounds the app for more than 5 minutes
